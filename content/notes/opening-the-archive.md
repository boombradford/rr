This is the first entry in a working log. The portfolio above carries the shipped work; this is the space behind it, where I write down what I am building while I am building it.

The posts will be short and frequent rather than long and rare. Notes on systems I am putting together (AI products, media tools, the connective tissue around them), what is working and what is not, references I am chasing, and the occasional longer piece when a project asks for one.

The format is markdown, the surface is plain HTML, no tracking, no analytics, no comments. The RSS feed lives at `/notes/rss.xml` for anyone who wants to follow along. Otherwise, this is mostly a place for me to think out loud and for the work to leave a trail.
