# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Creative technologists, founders, media operators, product teams, and collaborators evaluating AI infrastructure, media intelligence, product tooling, and distribution systems.

## Product Purpose

The site should feel credible, technically deep, visually tasteful, and already shipping. Success means a visitor understands the overlap between AI infrastructure, media intelligence, creative technology, and distribution within the first viewport, then sees enough live work to trust the claim.

## Brand Personality

Calm, analytical, cinematic. The interface should feel like a quiet intelligence archive rather than a generic portfolio template or a sci-fi dashboard.

## Anti-references

Avoid cyberpunk clutter, neon dashboards, fake terminal theatrics, default SaaS proof strips, generic icon-card grids, glassmorphism for decoration, purple-blue AI gradients, and portfolio pages that read like a template with project links swapped in.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with signal, not decoration.
2. Let shipped work carry credibility.
3. Make technical depth feel editorial and composed.
4. Keep motion purposeful, slow, and respectful of reduced-motion users.
5. Use one restrained accent and a warm dark material system.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Preserve visible focus states, semantic headings, descriptive link labels, sufficient contrast, reduced-motion behavior, mobile touch targets, and readable line lengths.
